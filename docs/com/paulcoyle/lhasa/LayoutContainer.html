Classpublic class LayoutContainer
InheritanceLayoutContainer LayoutElement flash.display.Sprite
SubclassesStageLayoutContainer

A LayoutContainer is responsible for any children within it that subclass LayoutElement and their positioning and sizing.

LayoutContainers delegate the actual task of performing the layout calculations to their layoutDelegate. Layout delegates can be changed at run-time.
